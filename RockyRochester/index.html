Meet The Band!

This is the coolest band you will ever listen to! Rock on with them in Rochester New York. Go to the events tab to see upcoming events.

        
Erin

Erin is the lead singer of Rocky Rochester. She grew up singing constantly and went to college to major in music. After finishing school she joined the Rocky Rochesters and would continue to live her dream.

John

John is the guitar player in the Rocky Rochester. He grew up learning guitar from his father who would constantly teach him new songs. After high school John got married and had two kids Lucy and Anna. He loved to write songs and play guitar for them and eventually those songs he originally wrote for them turned into the formation of a band.

Jen

Jen is a background singer and steps up as lead for a few songs that she wrote. Jen met John when he was about to have his second child and listened to his songs. She then encouraged him to play guitar and make a band with her and few other people she knew, Erin and Mark, so that they could touch the hearts of hundreds of kids.

Mark

Mark is the drummist of the Rocky Rochester. He met Jen in music class as they were both going to college together and Jen told Mark about starting a band and convinced him to join. From that day forward it was the best decision Mark says that he has ever made.

Meet Rocky The Raccoon!

The Official Unofficial Mascot of Rochester, NY

Rocky Rochester

Rocky The Raccoon is the mascot for Rocky Rochester and makes an appearances at every concert

History

Rocky The Raccoon began as the Rocky Rochester's mascot when most of the band met in college. They would stay up late and go out on car rides. Once they started thinking about creating a band John the guitarist spotted a raccoon outside Nick Tahou Hots that the four members always ate at. He instantly created a children's song based on that raccoon and since it described their band so much from their nocturnal behavior and eating garbage plates they thought it would be the best mascot that represented them as a band and something fun to remind them of their days together as students.
